Social media influencers are not solely responsible for promoting unrealistic beauty standards. Instead, they reflect and amplify societal pressure to conform to certain beauty norms. The emphasis on physical appearance in their content is often a result of the cultural values placed on beauty and attractiveness, which can be seen across various platforms, including social media. Furthermore, the impact of social media influencers on beauty standards is not necessarily negative, as many use their platforms to promote body positivity, self-acceptance, and diversity. Ultimately, it is crucial for individuals to critically evaluate the content they consume and develop a nuanced understanding of what beauty means to them.
